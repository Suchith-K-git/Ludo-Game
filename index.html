Ludo — 2 Player

Turn: Red
Dice: -

How to play

  • Click Roll Dice, then click a highlighted token to move.
  • Roll a 6 to bring a token out from base.
  • 6 gives an extra turn.
  • Landing on an opponent sends them to base (start squares are safe).
  • Exact roll to enter Home.
Valid tokens glow after you roll.
